Apple Pies

This was my grandmother's apple pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour to form a paste, then add water, white sugar, and brown sugar. Boil, reduce heat, and simmer.
  3. Place the bottom crust in the pan, fill with apples, and cover with a lattice crust.
  4. Pour the sugar and butter mixture over the crust slowly.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F (220°C), then reduce temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake 35-45 minutes until apples are soft.
